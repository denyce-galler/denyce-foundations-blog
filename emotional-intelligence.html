Cultural blogpost - Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to recognise and control one’s own emotions and also display awareness and understanding of other people’s emotions.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is a measure of one’s mental intelligence which differs from EQ as EQ is a measure of one’s emotional intelligence.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is considered essential in not only one’s ability to create relationships but it is also critical in being able to cultivate and contribute to a positive work setting.
